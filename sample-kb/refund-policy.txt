Acme accepts returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in their original packaging with all tags attached.

To start a return, visit your account dashboard and click "Return item" next to the order. You will receive a prepaid return label by email within 24 hours.

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method within 5 to 10 business days after we receive and inspect the item.

The following items are non-returnable: digital downloads, perishable goods, custom-engraved products, and gift cards.

If your item arrived damaged or defective, please contact support within 7 days of delivery and we will send a replacement at no charge.

For exchanges, please return the original item and place a new order. We do not offer direct item-for-item exchanges.
