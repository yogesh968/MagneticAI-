Standard shipping takes 3 to 5 business days. Express shipping takes 1 to 2 business days and costs $12.99 flat. Overnight shipping is available for $24.99 and delivers the next business day if ordered before 2 PM EST.

Free standard shipping is available on all orders over $50.

Tracking details are sent to your email address within 1 hour of your order shipping. You can also track your order from the Orders section in your account dashboard.

We ship to all 50 US states and to over 30 countries internationally. International orders typically arrive within 7 to 14 business days and may be subject to customs fees.

If your package is delayed or lost, please contact support and we will open an investigation with the carrier within 1 business day.
